About Developer

Experion Developers

Experion is a 100% FDI-funded real estate developer, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Experion Holding Pte. Limited, Singapore — the real estate arm of AT Holdings group of companies. Since 2006, they've built 9 landmark projects across Gurugram, Lucknow, Noida and Amritsar covering 6.8 million sq.ft.

Experion Group has presence across Haryana, Punjab, Uttar Pradesh, Telangana, Goa and Maharashtra — developing large-format townships, high-rise condominiums, commercial spaces, hotels and resorts.
